# Crema - find great coffee near you

Crema is a free coffee-shop finder that surfaces great independent cafes and hidden gems near you, with real Google ratings, photos, opening hours, and one-tap directions. No sign-up, nothing to install.

Live site: https://sipcrema.com

Crema finds highly-rated coffee shops near any location, highlights independent spots and hidden gems rather than chains, and shows real photos, hours, price level, and directions. It works anywhere: type a city or use your current location.

City guides - honest, local guides to drinking well in great coffee cities: Miami (https://sipcrema.com/miami/), Austin (https://sipcrema.com/austin/), Seattle (https://sipcrema.com/seattle/), Portland (https://sipcrema.com/portland/), and New York (https://sipcrema.com/new-york/).

How it is built: a single-file React app (React 18 via CDN), powered by the Google Places API (New) with an OpenStreetMap free fallback, hosted on Netlify with free HTTPS.

Made by a coffee lover who travels. Find your next great cup at https://sipcrema.com
